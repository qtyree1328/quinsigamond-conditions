How current is this?

Where each number comes from

This hub blends three very different update speeds. Knowing which is which keeps the readings in context.

Live
Weather & outlet river flow Air, wind, sun from Open-Meteo and river discharge from the USGS gauge — updated through the day.
Periodic
In-water lab samples Clarity, oxygen, phosphorus, pH and more — collected by hand on monitoring trips, sometimes months or years apart. Each sample shows its date.
Yearly
State of the Lake reports The City's full season summary and ratings, once a year. Read the 2022–2025 digest →
Not yet public
In-lake buoys Worcester's buoys measure temperature, clarity and algae every 30 minutes, but the live feed isn't publicly available yet.
